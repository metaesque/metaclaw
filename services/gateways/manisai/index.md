# manisai: Manis AI

## Overview

Manis AI is a no-code portal and workflow automation tool focused primarily on
accelerating app development and strategic planning. While it incorporates AI
elements to assist with presentation structuring, design, and logic flows, it
functions more as an intelligent builder than a traditional autonomous agent
gateway.

The platform allows users to leverage AI to quickly generate internal tools,
automate operational tasks, and design software architectures without writing
code. It integrates directly into the software development lifecycle, helping
teams move from a prompt to a deployed application or structured business plan
in a fraction of the customary time.

For individuals looking to build autonomous software agents that execute
background tasks in a terminal, Manis AI is not the right tool. It is highly
optimized for project planning, visual workflow creation, and rapid prototyping,
making it a valuable asset for product managers and designers rather than
backend engineers.
