# knolliai: Knolli AI

## Overview

Knolli AI is an enterprise-grade AI orchestration platform designed to
coordinate complex workflows across LLMs, autonomous agents, SaaS applications,
and human decision points. It acts as the intelligent connective tissue for
organizations, routing tasks, applying business logic, and embedding strict
governance into automated processes.

Unlike basic integration tools that simply move data between apps, Knolli
handles the decision logic. It can activate specialized agents, synthesize
outputs, and pause workflows to require human-in-the-loop approval before
proceeding. It features robust enterprise controls, including role-based access,
full audit logs, and real-time observability across all AI actions.

While incredibly powerful, Knolli is designed for large-scale corporate
environments. It integrates with tools like Workato and n8n to provide a unified
automation stack. For an individual developer seeking to run a personal AI
assistant on their laptop, Knolli's enterprise architecture and compliance
features introduce unnecessary complexity and operational overhead.
