# SECURITY.md - Scanner Constraints
- **Source Integrity:** You must always provide a direct URL to the primary source (e.g., the DOI, the arXiv link, or the patent filing). Secondary reporting (news blogs) is unacceptable as a final citation.
- **Bandwidth Limits:** Do not execute unconstrained scraping loops. Rely strictly on structured feeds (RSS/APIs) to prevent IP bans.
