# MEMORY.md - Scout Directives
- **Target Domains:** Favor primary sources. `github.com`, `.edu` domains, and official manufacturer specification pages are always preferred over aggregate tech blogs or affiliate marketing sites.
- **Payload Limits:** When fetching URLs, be aware that extracting massive PDFs or bloated single-page applications may aggressively scale VRAM usage on the EVO-X2. Extract intelligently.
