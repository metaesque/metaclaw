## Google

### Google AI Pro

#### Usage limits

How usage limits are calculated changed on May 17, 2026 per https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/16275805.
- limits refresh every 5 hours
- standard limits apply with unpaid plan
- AI Plus gives 2x standard
- AI Pro gives 4x standard
- AI Ultra gives 5-20x standard

Standard pricing involves "compute-based usage limits that determine how
much you can interact with Gemini tools and features. These limits factor in the
complexity of your prompt, the models and features you use, and the length of
your chat. Your limit refreshes every 5 hours until you reach your weekly
limit."

In the gemini.google.com UI, in Settings -> Usage Limits:
 - a single Gemini 3.1 Pro + Deep Research + Extended Thinking consumes 84% of 5 hour quota
